## 🧠 What is this?

**A small awareness mod that helps you keep control of the situation.**

It adds a compact segmented marker around the crosshair when a nearby threat is just outside your view.

---

## ✨ Why use it?

This mod is for players who:

* want a little more control over nearby danger
* prefer subtle QoL over loud HUD elements
* want something that still feels close to vanilla
* get startled by sudden zombie hits or creeper explosions

It is especially useful if sudden off-screen hits make you jump and you want a calmer warning before the damage happens.

---

## ⚙️ Features

* Compact segmented warning marker near the crosshair
* Tracks the nearest valid off-screen threat
* Supports nearby hostiles, approaching hostiles, and off-screen players
* Optional directional ping when the marker first appears
* Detection radius configurable from `6` to `24` blocks, default `12`
* Configurable marker radius and player filters
* Survival-only mode
* Mod Menu settings integration
* Client-side only

---

## 🎮 Controls

* `Open Attention Settings` keybind, unbound by default
* `/attention settings`
* `/attention reload`
* `/attention demo on`
* `/attention demo off`
* `/attention demo pulse`
* `/attention demo angle <degrees>`

## ❓ FAQ

### Does it work on servers?

Yes. It is a client-side mod.

---

### Do other players need to install it?

No.

---

### Does it only warn about off-screen threats?

Yes. The marker is meant for awareness outside your current view.

---

### Is it meant to stay vanilla-friendly?

Yes. It is a QoL mod, not a gameplay overhaul.

---

### Can I disable player warnings?

Yes. You can disable them completely or filter them with the player list.

---

### Where is the config stored?

`config/attention.json`
